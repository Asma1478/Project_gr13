group events 2012
≥ onninen group’s profitability improved considerably in 2012. the positive development was enabled by the clear
		 improvement of the profitability of onninen’s business operations in finland.

≥ onninen oy acquired the share capital of lämpö-tukku oy from panostaja oyj in spring 2012. the acquisition
		 improved further onninen’s strong competence in the wholesale of heating, plumbing and ventilation products
		 in the finnish markets.

≥ conny dyrenäs started his work as the managing director of onninen sweden in august 2012.

≥ in 2012, onninen russia continued to increase its already strong project business operations and, for example,
		 signed an agreement with the olympic winter games in sochi to provide a snow-making system for the 2014 winter olympics.

≥ moreover, onninen russia started deliveries for the major project in the st. petersburg waterworks
		 (vodokanal) in the latter part of 2012.

≥ the sap system rollout project continued as planned and onninen russia joined the group’s joint enterprise
		 resource planning (erp) system in the beginning of january 2013.
