prudent and responsible operations
kiatnakin phatra financial group focuses on operating with respect to rules and regulations,
not using business practices that can harm the economy, culture, society or environment
and not making profit to the detriment of society.

ผสานประโยชน์ของผู้มีส่วนได้ส่วนเสีย
พร้อมพัฒนาตลาดเงินและตลาดทุนไทยให้แข็งแกร่ง
กลุ่มธุรกิจการเงินเกียรตินาคินภัทร ยึดมั่นในการประสานประโยชน์ขององค์กร พนักงาน ลูกค้า และผู้ถือหุ้นให้สอดคล้องกับประโยชน์

co-ordinating the benefits/interests of stakeholders
while developing and strengthening thailand’s
money and capital markets
kiatnakin phatra financial group holds fast to the idea of aligning the interests of the organization,
employees, customers, and shareholders with the country’s economic development
and money and capital markets, focusing on growing along with the thai capital market.

แบ่งปันความรู้ ต่อยอดความคิด

using and sharing knowledge and expertise
for the benefit of all
kiatnakin phatra financial group encourages the use of the knowledge and expertise of the organization
as well as staff in the kiatnakin phatra financial group in areas such as management, system design
and law to be used for the benefit of society at large and public charities.

promoting volunteerism to give back to society
kiatnakin phatra financial group encourages its employees to volunteer for or to participate in
social activities and public charity events, providing funding to help as appropriate.
