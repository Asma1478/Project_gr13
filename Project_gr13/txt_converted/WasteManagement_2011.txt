waste management in summary as of december 31, 2010


waste management is the largest provider of comprehensive waste and environmental
services in north america, as well as north america’s largest municipal waste recycler
and a leader in waste-based energy technologies. headquartered in houston, texas,
the company is publicly traded (nyse:wm). we serve over 20 million customers with
environmentally sound management of solid wastes and transformation of waste
into usable resources.
